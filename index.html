The Tale...

There's a corruption in the air infecting the entire world and shrouding existence into a dark abyss. The corruption is hijacking the minds of the weak-willed, turning them into nightmarish, eldritch horrors, known as "VOIDS." It's up to a blonde heroine, by the name of Starlight, to fight off these evil beings and bring a total stop to The Corruption. But will she be able to do it alone? Or will she, too, become corrupted?
